Biography

I am currently a Senior Research Scientist with Huawei Technologies, working on Computer Vision and Machine Learning problems. Recent research directions include multi-task learning, computational photography and meta-learning.

Previously I held postdoctoral positions with the Biomedical Image Analysis Group, Imperial College London, working with Dr. Ben Glocker, Dr. Bernhard Kainz and Professor Daniel Rueckert and, prior to that, with Disney Research Zurich working on real-time facial performance capture. I earned my PhD at the University of Edinburgh in the Institute of Perception, Action and Behaviour under the supervision of Prof. Bob Fisher for my work on density estimation and multi-view 3D point set registration.

My research interests span topics in Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Medical Image Analysis. You can occasionally find me up a mountain; running or cycling.
